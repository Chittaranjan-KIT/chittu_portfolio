Ahmed Body - External Aerodynamics

Steady-state RANS simulation of external flow over a standard Ahmed body at 40 m/s using OpenFOAM.

+
Description

End-to-end CFD simulation of external flow over an Ahmed body at 40 m/s. Mesh generated via blockMesh and snappyHexMesh (3.35M cells). Solved with simpleFoam and k-omega SST turbulence model. Post-processed in ParaView for pressure, velocity, streamlines, and wake structures.

Key Parameters
  • Freestream velocity: 40 m/s | Re = 2.75 x 10^6
  • Turbulence model: k-omega SST
  • Mesh: 3.35M cells, hex-dominant
  • Solver: simpleFoam, 500 iterations

Rotary Eddy Current Brake - Feasibility & Performance Analysis

Multi-physics simulation: electromagnetic torque modelling (Python), structural FEM, and thermal CFD.

+
Overview

Feasibility study of eddy current brakes (ECB) - contactless, wear-free, and particulate-free braking technology addressing EU non-exhaust emission regulations. Three simulation pillars: electromagnetic modelling, structural FEM, and thermal CFD.

Electromagnetic FEM using COMSOL

Validation of the Wouterse analytical model through a 2D axisymmetric electromagnetic FEM analysis in COMSOL Multiphysics 6.4. Maximum calculated braking torque = 410 N·m at 641 RPM for a disc made of Al 6061 with B₀ = 0.8 T.

Automotive Engineering AI Brain - Local RAG System

End-to-end local, private, and completely free RAG system for Automotive Engineering, following the viral idea suggested by Andrej Karpathy (co-founder of OpenAI).

+
Overview

Created an end-to-end local, private, and completely free RAG system for Automotive Engineering, following the viral idea suggested by Andrej Karpathy (co-founder of OpenAI). The difference from regular AI chatbots is that my system provides responses exclusively based on curated automotive engineering knowledge.

Why This Project Is Unique

Standard language models such as ChatGPT or Claude are trained on internet datasets. My project addresses this by creating a personal knowledge base searched by AI prior to providing any response - just like enterprise RAG systems do.

What Was Done

Creation of an end-to-end knowledge base covering 16 areas of automotive engineering including chassis engineering, vehicle dynamics, CFD, CAE/FEA, EV fundamentals, NVH, ADAS, thermal management, suspension engineering, lightweighting, simulation methods, aerodynamics, interior design, powertrain engineering, and manufacturing engineering.

Pipeline
  • Engineering documents (.md) → Embedding in the vector knowledge base
  • User poses an engineering question
  • Knowledge base retrieval of relevant chunks
  • AI gives a response based only on domain knowledge
  • Contextualized answer, with citations from documents
Results Achieved
  • Full embedding of 16 knowledge domains
  • Successful testing of RAG knowledge retrieval
  • Beautiful visualization of knowledge graph
  • 100% privacy - no data transmitted to any cloud server
  • Free of charge - everything happens locally
